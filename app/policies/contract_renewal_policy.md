# Contract Renewal Policy

## Automatic Renewal

Contracts marked as "active" may automatically renew if a renewal_date is defined and no termination notice is submitted 30 days prior to renewal.

## Manual Renewal Review

Contracts with a contract_value greater than 200000 require manual executive review before renewal approval.

## Renewal Risk Indicators

A contract is considered high renewal risk if:
- The assigned employee has employment_status = terminated
- The vendor has had prior compliance issues
- The contract_status is pending_renewal for more than 60 days

## Renewal Timing

Renewal notifications should be sent 45 days before the renewal_date.
